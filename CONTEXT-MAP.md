# Context Map

## Contexts

- [Climb](./docs/domain/climb/CONTEXT.md) — the mortal journey through the Spire, including combat, routes, bargains, and its ending
- [Vigil](./docs/domain/vigil/CONTEXT.md) — the between-climb memory that reveals systems, records quests, and assembles Emberglass

## Relationships

- **Climb → Vigil**: a Climb ends with a Run Outcome and carries earned progress into the Vigil.
- **Vigil → Climb**: the Vigil reveals systems and Emberglass Quests that can alter later Climbs.
- **Climb ↔ Emberglass Quest**: a Quest is remembered by the Vigil but advanced through discoveries and acts within a Climb.
