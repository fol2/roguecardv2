# Event Taxonomy Prototype Prompt Ledger

These prompts produced the approved Round 4 event-art pitch scenes. Keep them as
the baseline for future event-art production prompts.

## Approved Direction

Round 4 resolves the gap between Round 3's strong subject read and the event
screen's need for narrative. The working contract is:

- foreground: chunky main subject with the strongest contour and contrast
- midground: one or two story props that clarify the choice
- background: two or three broad environment shapes
- background contrast and line count lower than the subject
- no detailed lattice, floor grid, or busy architecture

## Prompt - Wounded Knight, Round 4

```text
Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: Spirebound event art pitch prototype, Round 4 narrative background demo
Primary request: The Wounded Knight, a priced encounter event: a crushed stained-glass knight slumped in the foreground, holding out a small glowing relic; behind him, one broken pillar and a simple cathedral arch silhouette suggest the place where he fell.
Round 4 direction: keep the successful R3 chunky subject stroke, but add a narrative background with depth. Foreground: the knight and relic. Midground: one broken pillar and fallen banner. Background: two or three huge soft stained-glass arch shapes, blurred and low-detail. The subject and background should feel dimensional and layered, not flat, but the background must stay broad and simple.
Narrative read: show the wounded figure first, the offered relic second, and the lonely ruined chapel setting third. It must read inside a 440px event panel and still scan at 128px.
Style/medium: serious cartoon-gothic stained-glass game art; sturdy event illustration; chunky black outer contour on the main subject; 3-5 large jewel-tone colour masses on the subject; background uses broad soft stained-glass planes, not fine lattice; thick black lead dividers used sparingly; matte painterly glass texture; warm amber relic glow; controlled blue visor glow.
Composition/framing: wide 3:2 full event scene; centre-right knight focal; foreground/midground/background depth; generous breathing room; no hard card frame; no cropped knight, relic, pillar, or arch.
Lighting/mood: readable gothic decision-scene mood; luminous midtones; warm relic light against cool blue ruined chapel light; background lower contrast than subject.
Palette: luminous bruised blue glass, pale silver-blue chapel light, black lead, small warm amber relic light, one muted red wound pane.
Constraints: no text; no labels; no watermark; no buttons; no UI chrome; no full card mockup; no extra characters; avoid thin strokes; avoid many cracks; avoid fragile glass filigree; avoid detailed window lattice; avoid floor tile grid; avoid busy background architecture; background must have fewer lines than the subject.
```

Lighting/mood: readable gothic danger mood; luminous midtones; warm lock light against cool violet-blue treasury light; background lower contrast than chest.

Lighting/mood: readable solemn craft mood; luminous midtones; bright forge heat against cool blue-black chamber light; background lower contrast than anvil and card pane.
