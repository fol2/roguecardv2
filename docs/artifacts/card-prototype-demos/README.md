## Decision Record

Round 1 failed because the sheets were too dark and the rarity ladder did not
pass. The shield variants felt like repeated panels instead of distinct rarity
tiers.

Round 2 is the approved direction: brighter review ground, higher-value jewel
glass, larger silhouettes, no black-on-black details, and rarity escalation
through silhouette, material, halo strength, arch structure, and ceremony.

The prompts used for Round 2 are stored in
[`prompt-ledger.md`](./prompt-ledger.md).
