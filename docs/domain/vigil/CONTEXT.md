# Vigil

The Vigil is the memory between Climbs: it reveals systems, records deeds and quests, and gathers the Rose Window.

## Language

**Vigil**:
The persistent between-climb record of discoveries, deeds, monuments, Vows, and Emberglass.
_Avoid_: Meta screen, profile, account

**Reveal**:
A permanent disclosure that makes a system or content pool available on later Climbs.
_Avoid_: Feature flag, unlock animation

**Emberglass Quest**:
One of six long-form discoveries whose completion lights a pane of the Rose Window.
_Avoid_: Mission, achievement, objective

**Dormant**:
An Emberglass Quest that is not yet discoverable or active.
_Avoid_: Locked, hidden

**Armed**:
An Emberglass Quest that can be encountered but has not yet disclosed its identity.
_Avoid_: Enabled, active

**Revealed**:
An Emberglass Quest whose identity and progress are visible in the Vigil.
_Avoid_: Unlocked, active

**Complete**:
An Emberglass Quest whose final condition has been met and whose Shard has joined the Rose Window.
_Avoid_: Done, claimed

**Trail**:
An Emberglass Quest advanced by repeated discoveries across one or more Climbs.
_Avoid_: Counter quest, grind quest

**Gate**:
An Emberglass Quest resolved by meeting one decisive condition after its setup is complete.
_Avoid_: Boss quest, binary quest

**Emberglass Shard**:
The permanent piece earned by completing one Emberglass Quest; six Shards complete the Rose Window.
_Avoid_: Token, fragment, pane

**Dawn Ceremony**:
The ordered post-Climb disclosure of whispers, quest changes, pages, Shards, and the sealed summit promise.
_Avoid_: Results queue, end screen

**Whisper**:
A short piece of between-climb lore that foreshadows Emberglass without advancing a Quest.
_Avoid_: Hint, notification

**Rose Window**:
The six-pane Vigil image assembled from Emberglass Shards and completed before the sealed summit promise appears.
_Avoid_: Progress wheel, quest board

**Act IV Promise**:
The sealed-summit disclosure queued after all six Emberglass Shards complete the Rose Window, then staged to one Dawn Ceremony without creating a playable Act IV.
_Avoid_: Act IV victory, fourth Act
