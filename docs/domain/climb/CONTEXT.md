# Climb

A Climb is one mortal journey through the Spire, from embarkation until dawn, death, or abandonment.

## Language

**Climb**:
A single journey through the Spire with one Aspect, deck, and route.
_Avoid_: Session, campaign, playthrough

**Dawn**:
The successful end of a Climb after the Sovereign is defeated.
_Avoid_: Victory screen, run win

**Fall**:
The end of a Climb by death, leaving a monument that a later Climb may recover.
_Avoid_: Game over, failed run

**Abandonment**:
The deliberate end of an unfinished Climb without reaching dawn or falling in combat.
_Avoid_: Quit, cancellation

**Run Outcome**:
The mutually exclusive conclusion of a Climb: dawn, fall, or abandonment.
_Avoid_: Result code, terminal state

**Unlit Way**:
A hidden route whose keeper and cost are disclosed only when the climber enters it.
_Avoid_: Secret node, mystery path

**Hollow Bargain**:
One of the five prices offered by the Hollow Lamplighter along the Unlit Way.
_Avoid_: Transaction, purchase
