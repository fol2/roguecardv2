## Cleaned candidates 03 and 04

Both candidates passed the six-sector, quest-order, sealed-door, stair, palette, and thumbnail-readability gates. They were rejected because the Eighth Omen pane retained eight pale-gold marks plus the black breaking stroke, rather than seven pale marks with the black stroke as the eighth. Candidate 04 showed that the broad Nano Banana edit did not remove the extra mark. The final correction therefore used a built-in precise-object edit against candidate 04.

## Cleaned candidate 05

Rejected at the deterministic composite gate. Its content was semantically correct, but the mural carried its own full-bleed outer rim, radial lead, and oversized central boss. The real masks clipped important subject parts, the deterministic frame produced double lines, and the boss hid the door and stair at medallion scale. It was superseded by the frameless geometry edit and centred 700 px placement.
